# Portfolio Tone Guide

## Communication Style

- Direct and concise — no fluff
- Shows, doesn't tell — let the work demonstrate capability
- Bilingual — Bahasa Indonesia for clients, English for code and docs
- Self-deprecating humor when appropriate
- No buzzwords or hype — states what things do, not what they "revolutionize"

## What I'm Not

- Not a "just frontend" developer — I deploy, I script, I manage databases
- Not a tutorial-project developer — everything I build has real users or solves a real problem
- Not corporate — professional but approachable, warm but direct
- Not a job hopper — I build depth at each role, not just collect titles

## Writing Rules for Portfolio Copy

- Use first person ("I built..." not "Fahrurrozy built...")
- Be specific about impact (numbers, time saved, users served)
- Lead with the problem, not the tech
- Keep it conversational but professional
- Show personality through project choices and fun facts, not through trying to be funny in copy
- Let the case studies do the heavy lifting — the about section should be brief and punchy
