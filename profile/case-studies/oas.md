# OAS (Oassyst.com) — Paperless Competition Judging

## My Role
Co-founder & Developer. Built the platform from the ground up — product vision, development, and delivery.

## Context
Paskibra (marching/flag ceremony) competitions in Indonesian schools involve dozens of teams, multiple judges, and complex scoring across multiple criteria. These events are organized by schools and community groups.

## Problem
The entire process — from team registration to judge scoring to final results — was done on paper. Score sheets got lost, manual calculations took until dawn (literally — organizers reported working until subuh/early morning), human errors crept in, and results lacked transparency. Parents and teams had no visibility into scores until the final announcement.

## Solution
Built **OAS (Online Assignment System)** — a fully digital, paperless competition platform:
- Digital registration (no paper forms)
- Live scoring by judges via mobile app
- Automatic score calculation and ranking
- Real-time score visibility for parents and teams
- NPP (performance order) assignment
- Event configuration flexible enough for various competition types
- Transparent, auditable results

## Tech
Laravel, Inertia.js, Vue, Tailwind CSS, Filament admin panel

## Result
- Time savings of up to **12 hours per event** (no more manual recaps until dawn)
- Used by **hundreds of schools** across Indonesia
- Judges can focus on judging, not paperwork
- Parents can watch scores in real-time on their phones
- Complete transparency eliminates scoring disputes
